Article Printing & Packaging

Article Label & Accessories Ltd., R.P.H. Label & Accessories, and Article Printing & Packaging are proud sister concerns of R.PH. Group — a leading garments accessories manufacturer based in Dhaka, Bangladesh.

Since its establishment in 2003, R.P.H. Group has been dedicated to producing a wide range of premium-quality garment trims and packaging items, catering to both local and international apparel brands. Our product line includes woven labels, printed labels, hang tags, barcode tags, photo inlays, adhesive stickers, leather and PU patches, silicon/rubber patches, printed canvas tapes, heat transfer labels, high-density prints, and printed self-adhesive poly bags, among many others.

Under the visionary leadership of Managing Director Md. Hanif Mondal, the group has earned a strong reputation for technical excellence, reliability, and innovation. Environmental responsibility is a core principle of our operations — we strictly comply with Bangladesh's environmental regulations and prioritize eco-friendly materials and sustainable production practices.

R.P.H. Group is an active member of the Bangladesh Garments Accessories & Packaging Manufacturers & Exporters Association (BGAPMEA) and DCCI (since 2018), demonstrating our continuous commitment to industry standards and ongoing improvement.

Our facilities are located at 16/D, Chanpara, Uzampur, Uttarkhan, Uttara, Dhaka-1230. For more information please visit our official website: www.rphgroupbd.com

Our Journey

Our journey through the years

From a small label unit in 2003 to a certified, export-ready accessories manufacturer — every year added a new chapter to our story. Here are the milestones that shaped who we are today.

Our mission goes beyond labels — it's about empowering people, industry and the nation's backbone RMG sector.

2003

Foundation Year

Founded in 2003, Article Label & Accessories Ltd. grew from our early journey with R.PH. Label (1997). Under the leadership of Ms. Israt Jahan Merry and Mr. Hanif Mondol, the company has become a trusted name in Bangladesh's garment accessories sector.

2005

Expansion in Woven & Printed Label

We expanded our production by introducing woven and printed label manufacturing — a major milestone in our growth. This step enhanced our product range and laid a strong foundation for future innovations in label solutions.

2008

Started Offset Production

We introduced German Heidelberg offset printing machines to our production line, boosting our capabilities. This upgrade improved quality and opened new possibilities for diverse labels and accessories.

2017

Introducing Digital Printing

We introduced digital printing to our production line — delivering fast, sharp and long-lasting prints. With no plates or setup required, it became our best solution for variable data printing such as barcodes, QR codes and serial numbers, along with short-run jobs and instant proofs.

2020

Global Standard Achievements

We proudly achieved OEKO-TEX®, FSC® and GRS certifications, marking a major milestone in our journey. These certifications reflect our strong commitment to safe chemical use, sustainable practices, and environmental responsibilities.

2025

Expanding Global Presence

Building on our strong local foundation, we began expanding into the global market, bringing our products to customers around the world. This move shows our commitment to growth, quality, and serving customers worldwide.

2026

Narrow Fabric & PU Patch Production Start

We started dedicated narrow fabric and PU patch production, expanding our in-house capabilities and giving customers even more trims from a single reliable source.

2026

Automatic 3 Color Screen Print

We added an automatic 3 color screen printing machine to our production line — printing up to three colours in a single automated pass with precise registration. This delivers vibrant, durable prints at higher speed and consistent quality, batch after batch.

2027

Introducing RFID

We are introducing RFID technology to our labels and accessories — enabling smarter inventory tracking and connected products for global brands.
